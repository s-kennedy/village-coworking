More Information

  • Location TBD: We're still working on securing a space near downtown Kitchener. The location will be easily accessible from public transit.
  • Childcare conditions: The childcare service is intended for members of the coworking who will be working on-site. This is not a licensed daycare. Registered ECEs will be available to provide childcare, but parents must remain in the office.
  • Childminding exchange: Members are encouraged to exchange childminding with each other and use the childcare space. Parents must stay on-site however, and are always responsible for their own children.

If you have any questions or just want to chat, feel free to contact us.
