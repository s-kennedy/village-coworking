Childcare

On-site childcare on demand

On-site childcare is available from Monday to Friday to all members at the same rates.

  • $50 per day (9am to 5pm)
  • $30 per half day (9am to 1pm or 1pm to 5pm)
  • $15 per hour

Members are encouraged to participate in our babysitting exchange to reduce their childcare costs. Ask to join our Slack group to chat with other parents who are willing to trade babysitting hours.

Skillshare

Contribute your skills and save on cost

Your time and skills are valuable. We have a few volunteer roles available with a time commitment of about 5 hours per week. In exchange, you'll get a 20% discount on your membership or one free day pass per week.

In the spirit of "it takes a village", we believe that our community will be better and stronger with the active participation of our members.

If you are interested in one of these roles or if you want to participate in the skillshare in another way, please reach out.

We have the following roles to fill:

  • Social media specialist
  • Marketing and communications specialist
  • Playspace maintainer
  • Toy library manager
  • On-call childcare support
  • Partnerships developer
  • Logistics and admin support

More Information

  • Location TBD: We're still working on securing a space near downtown Kitchener. The location will be easily accessible from public transit.
  • Childcare conditions: The childcare service is intended for members of the coworking who will be working on-site. It is open to children up to five years old. This is not a licensed daycare. Registered ECEs will be available to provide childcare, but parents must remain in the office.
  • Babysitting exchange: Members are encouraged to exchange babysitting with each other and use the play space. Parents must stay on-site however, and are always responsible for their own children.
  • Facility maintenance: The office will be professionally cleaned on a weekly basis and the play space and toys will be cleaned and sterilised at the end of every day.

If you have any questions or just want to chat, feel free to contact us.
